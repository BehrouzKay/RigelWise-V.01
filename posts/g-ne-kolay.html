Overview

GüneşKolay sought to expand their innovative DIY renewable energy solutions into the United Kingdom market. As demonstrated in solar pannel.jpg, their product offers homeowners a seamless way to harness solar power. However, entering the UK required more than just a great product; they needed a comprehensive roadmap for legal business establishment, brand identity development, and a complex logistical network to handle international distribution and local sales.

Our Strategic Solution
Rigel Wise served as the primary growth partner for GüneşKolay, managing every facet of their market entry:

Business Foundation: We facilitated the complete management of their business establishment in the UK, ensuring all regulatory requirements were met.

Brand Identity: We led the development of their Brandbook, ensuring their visual and verbal identity was perfectly calibrated for the UK consumer.

Logistical Excellence: We optimized their warehousing operations, creating a highly efficient and cost-effective system to manage inventory flow.

Market Penetration: We provided end-to-end consultancy for local marketing campaigns and regulated their entire sales process across the EU, ensuring a smooth customer journey and scalable operations.

The Impact
Through our holistic approach, GüneşKolay successfully launched its presence in the UK. By integrating business infrastructure, strategic branding, and streamlined logistics, Rigel Wise empowered GüneşKolay to deliver their mission of making renewable energy accessible to every household with ease and efficiency.
