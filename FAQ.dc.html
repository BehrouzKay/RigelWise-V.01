(01)  Working with us

Do we have to take every service?+

No. Most clients begin with strategic consulting or business setup and add services as the expansion progresses. The advantage is that when you do add one, it is delivered by a team that already knows your business.

How are engagements priced?+

Consulting is typically fixed-fee by phase. Operations run on a monthly retainer scaled to activity. Venture-studio engagements can blend fees with an equity or success component. We agree the model before any work begins.

How long does a market entry take?+

A feasibility study takes three to six weeks. A full setup — entity, brand and digital presence — usually reaches trading readiness within three to five months, depending on sector and regulation.

Which markets do you cover?+

Our core is the United Kingdom and the European Union, in both directions — UK firms moving into Europe and European firms establishing in the UK. We take other regions selectively, through vetted local partners.

Will you sign an NDA?+

Always, and gladly. Discretion is central to how we work — a large share of our engagements are never publicly attributed to us at all.

Who will we actually work with?+

A partner leads every engagement personally. We keep our client list deliberately small so senior people stay close to the work rather than delegating it to a junior team.
