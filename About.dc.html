(01)  Who we are

Rigelwise began because expansion kept breaking at the seams between firms, the strategist who never saw the launch, the lawyer who never met the marketer. We built one studio that carries the whole arc.

Fourteen years on, we work quietly with healthcare groups, jewellers, property firms and hospitality names entering the UK and Europe, pairing venture-studio conviction with consulting rigour and hands-on operations.
